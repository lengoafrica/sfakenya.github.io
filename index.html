About Us

WHO ARE WE

SFA is an organistion that seeks to act as the enabler for building the next generation of relentless business changemakers in africa, who use entreprenuership and innovation as a tool to solve Africa's most pressing needs.

GOAL

To instigate the development of 10,000 business leaders in Africa over the next 10 years

  • Why us?

    Exclusive to teenagers we don't just offer training we track progress of our young teenage fellows and offer support required along the way.

  • How different are we?

    "We say it's okay to fail"

    At StartUp Front we believe that if we can encourage early failures when there is not much at stake, then we can create a culture that supports risk-takers who can actually change the world from a young age.

HOW DO WE ACHEIVE THIS?

We select talented teenagers from across the continent into our training program where we give them mentorship (from established founders who started young, preferably in their teenage so they can relate), access to our network, media publicity for achievements and possible funding opportunities.
